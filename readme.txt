SPromoter Social Reviews for WooCommerce helps you collect and display reviews from your customers on your WooCommerce store.

== Description ==

This plugin integrates seamlessly with the SPromoter platform, allowing you to leverage social proof to drive sales and grow your business. Automatically collect and display customer reviews, customize review display options, and enhance credibility and trust among potential buyers.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I display reviews on my product pages? =
Once the plugin is installed and activated, you have to configure the settings to connect your store with the SPromoter platform. Navigate to SPromoter from sidebar to configure the plugin settings. Once the settings are configured, reviews will be displayed on your product pages.

= Can I customize the appearance of the review widget? =
Yes, you can customize the appearance of the review widget to match your store's design. Go to the SPromoter website and you will find options to customize the appearance of the review widget.
